Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filled whith your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,dicev vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs whith a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over madium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny rn the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Enjoy:Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
